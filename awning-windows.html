Awning Windows

Top-hinged windows that open outward, allowing ventilation even during rain. Available from standard residential to heavy-duty commercial grades. Miami-Dade rated and ENERGY STAR ready.

67T Series

67T Series

Standard Residential Awning

The 67T Series Awning features self-lubricating stainless steel hinges (ASTM A276) supporting 150lbs with 50,000+ cycles. 9/16" glazing depth accommodates argon-filled IGUs (U ≤ 0.28, ENERGY STAR). Integrated magnetic fiberglass mesh retractable screen (AAMA 303) blocks 90% UV.

78T Series

Enhanced Residential Awning

The 78T Series offers deeper 78.3mm profile with 27mm argon cavity. Low maintenance anodized/powder-coated finishes resist corrosion. Modular designs fit openings from 14" to 48" width. Certified safety glazing (AS/NZS 4666) and 5,000+ open/close cycles (ASTM E283).

150T Series

Heavy-Duty Commercial Awning

The heavy-duty 150T Series has the deepest 153mm profile with 2.0mm wall thickness. NFRC U-factor 0.25 with dual Low-E coatings and warm-edge spacers. Heavy-duty stainless steel hinges (NFRC 100-2025) support 200lbs with 100,000-cycle durability. AAMA 2605-compliant frame withstands 150mph winds (ASTM E330).
