100 Series Pivot Door

The 100 Series Pivot Door features a substantial 102mm profile depth with 2.0mm wall thickness. The pivot mechanism allows the door to rotate on a central or offset axis, creating a dramatic architectural entrance. Available with double tempered glass for maximum security and thermal performance. Ideal for grand entries, modern homes, and commercial spaces.
