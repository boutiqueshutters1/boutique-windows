Sliding Doors

Expansive glass panels that glide effortlessly on dual tracks. Available in configurations up to 220 inches wide with silent rollers and advanced thermal break technology.

127T Series

The 127T Series features an unbreakable thermal shield engineered for extreme durability, high load capacity, and superior thermal insulation. Self-lubricating silent rollers with dual-bearing nylon wheels (200kg load) produce ≤35dB noise after 100,000 cycles (ASTM E413). Triple drainage system with hidden channels and pressure-equalized cavities (≤5ml/min leakage at 700Pa, AAMA 501.15). EPDM+TPE dual seals reduce U-value by 0.3, remaining flexible at -30°C (ASTM D412).

155T Series

The 155T Series is the premium sliding door with a massive 153mm profile depth and 2.0mm wall thickness. Seamless welding technology matches automobile-grade processes. 3-point locking with 4 anti-pick bolts (ANSI/BHMA A156.23 Grade 1 certified) withstands ≥500 lbs forced entry. 24mm polyamide thermal break barrier achieves U-value ≤1.6 (NFRC certified), 30% more efficient than Title 24.
